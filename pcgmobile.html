PopCultGang consists of Amalie Patricia, Mathias Bjørnskov & Maja "Blood Girl". We are a toy-collecting, retro game-loving, B-movie family, who made this place to share our passion with like-minded individuals. We're now aiming to create the best, coolest space for nostalgics like ourselves, with something that separates itself from the influx of vintage toy stores, by not only acting as a regular store, but also a community space, a time-machine and an experience both in our physical store and online!
